OTC Global Database

It accumulates all deals on the market and connects sellers and buyers from around the world.
Features
  • Swap non-listed tokens
    You can swap any tokens you want before listing them on exchange
  • No volume limits
    You can create any order with your own terms
  • Multiple participants during a single swap
    Up to 10 people can participate at the same swap
  • All trading pairs supported
    You can create any trading pair, so you don't need to do several swaps
  • Private and public swaps
    If you already know another part, you can create a private swap and share a link. It won't go to our main page
  • No sign-ups
    You don't have to sign up and pass verification to use our service
